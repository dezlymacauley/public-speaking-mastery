### What makes a point memorable

Start with an argument.
argument = a statement with support or elaboration

1. Claim = An assertion that you want the audience to believe.

2. Support = The evidence used to support and validate the claim.
This can be an example or a statistic. 
This gives the audience further reason on why they should believe you and
continue listening.

3. Warrant = This is what links the support to the claim.
