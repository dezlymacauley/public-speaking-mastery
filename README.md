# Public Speaking Mastery
_______________________________________________________________________________

## Why to I want to master public speaking?

1. Public speaking is an elite skill. Many people are either bad at public
speaking or would rather avoid it if given the choice. 
So I want to be part of the elite group of people who can confidently 
articulate their ideas on a public stage, 
and who willingly do this on a regular basis.

2. As much I like computers and programming, 
I don't want that to be my only skill. Public speaking is a skill on its own,
but it can also give me the power to explain technical concepts to 
non-technical people, or technical people who need to quickly brought up to
speed about a project or topic. 
There's a massive potential for this in tech sales and consulting.

3. I want to improve the spoken content 
of my [YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@dezlymacauley), by having
the skills to design well-written scripts that are delivered in manner that
will boost my personal brand or any product / service that I am promoting.
