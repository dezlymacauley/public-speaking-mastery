rhetoric = communication with the goal of influencing peoples choices on
a specific matter. 
Identify communication needs and strategically respond to them.

As a general rule: Keep it under 15 minutes, address the key concerns.

Then adapt based on these points

The topic => (What is the range of things I can talk about?)

Setting => of the speach (Where is the speach taking place?)

Audience => (What does the audience already know? 
What questions or reservations would they have)

Occasion => (What key points does your audience expect to hear for this kind
of speach? Then choose when to confirm or deviate from this)

Credibility => (Why should they listen to you? And how can you perform your
expertise in the speach) 

A good presentation is the result of picking the best strategy for each 
of these points.
